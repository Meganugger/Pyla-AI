About Pyla AI

Learn more about our advanced Brawl Stars automation bot

What is Pyla AI?

Pyla AI is a bot that plays Brawl Stars for you without you having to do anything. It can help you farm mastery and gain trophies up to a certain point.

See Pyla AI in Action

Video of Pyla AI in action

Features

  • Movement
  • Shooting with base and super attacks
  • Gadget and Hypercharge
  • Enemy Detection and Wall Detection (trained for 45 hours with 100,000+ images)
  • Automatically starts and finishes games for you
  • Unstuck Movement Delay
  • Unstuck Duration
  • Match History
  • Bot Customization - Max IPS, Gadgets, GPU/CPU, Crash Detection, Wall Detection Confidence, and more

Install Requirements

  • Computer running Windows 10/11
  • Python 3.11.9
  • LDPlayer/Bluestacks (LDPlayer recommended)

Available Gamemodes

  • 3v3 gamemodes
  • 5v5 gamemodes
